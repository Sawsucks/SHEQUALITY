VOLUNTEER

Check out 3 locations to volunteer. We will have more coming in the future so make sure to check back soon.

Nepal

  • 4 Week Summer Program
  • Help build schools
  • Tutor/Teach the young women on basic subjects
  • Support exercise and sports through weekend activities
  • Living and Eating Accomadations provided

Afghanistan

  • 8 Week Summer Program
  • Help build homes and areas of safety for women and young girls
  • Provide emotional support to young girls and women
  • Learn valuable skills from the women of Afghanistan
  • Take a trip to the Babur tomb or Band-e-Amir National Park for a weekend
  • Living and Eating Accommodations provided

Nigeria

  • 12 Week Summer Program
  • Educate women and girls about the political system
  • Host clubs and camps teaching sports to women and girls
  • Build a tech lab with solar panels to power it
  • Visit the Agodi Gardens or the Lekki Conservation Center for a weekend
  • Living and Eating Accommodations provided
